About Us

Company Focus

Pako Global Projects (PGP) is a versatile and innovative company offering a wide range of services to meet diverse client needs. Our focus is to provide exceptional solutions in construction, transportation, web development, and land clearance & maintenance.


In construction, we handle everything from plumbing and renovations to electrical installations, furnishing, and sourcing high-quality materials and equipments for our clients.


Our transportation services include moving goods and people, with trailer hiring options available to ensure flexible and reliable logistics.


Additionally, we specialize in web development, crafting modern, functional websites to help businesses thrive in the digital age.



Our land clearance and maintenance services cover everything from tree felling and demolition to grass cutting and professional cleaning, ensuring pristine environments for homes and offices.


At PGP, we are committed to delivering excellence, backed by a dedicated team of experts who bring experience, professionalism, and a customer-centric approach to every project.
